Swiggy Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Swiggy and its affiliates use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our websites, mobile applications, or other online properties (collectively, the "Services").

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help us enhance your user experience and provide us with information about how our Services are used.

Types of Cookies

We use various types of cookies, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Services.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how users interact with our Services.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used to personalize advertisements and measure their effectiveness.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated.
